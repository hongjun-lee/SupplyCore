📐 移动加权成本算法演示
入库时：
unit_cost_after = (qty_before × cost_before + qty_in × cost_in) / (qty_before + qty_in)
qty_after = qty_before + qty_in
出库时：
unit_cost_after = unit_cost_before // 出库不改单价
qty_after = qty_before - qty_out
amount_out = qty_out × unit_cost_before
S-21 流水必填字段：unit_cost_before/after, quantity_before/after, amount_before/after
